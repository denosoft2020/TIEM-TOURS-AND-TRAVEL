Our Story

From humble beginnings to creating extraordinary travel experiences

Founded in 2010

TIEM Tours began as a small team of travel enthusiasts with a shared passion for exploring the world and creating meaningful connections with different cultures. What started as a hobby among friends quickly grew into a mission to share these experiences with others.

Over the past decade, we've curated hundreds of tours across six continents, always focusing on authentic experiences, sustainable travel, and exceptional service. Our journey has been filled with incredible moments, from our first group tour to Bali to organizing luxury expeditions to Antarctica.
